About Us

Shri Someshwar Shikshan Prasarak Mandals Sharadchandra Pawar College of Engineering and Technology, Someshwarnagar established in 2012, is one of the self-financed Engineering colleges approved by AICTE New Delhi, Government of Maharashtra and DTE Mumbai. The institute is affiliated to Savitribai Phule Pune University (SPPU). The institute offers Engineering Courses in Mechanical Engineering, Civil Engineering, Computer Engineering and Electrical Engineering leading to BE degree as per SPPU University Curriculum.

Sharadchandra Pawar College of Engineering and Technology College has taken up a drive to provide students centric education, in order to produce industry ready engineers. Industry networking linkages, project or problem oriented approach, clarity in fundamental learning, exposure to industry through visits and expert lectures, self-development, guidance from institutes of higher learning and research.

Shri. Purushottam R. Jagtap

President

Shri Someshwar Shikshan Prasarak Mandal’s has been established in year 1972 to avail the education facility in rural area to the students and wards of stakeholders. Due to the increasing demand for Technical Courses, in year 2011 Sanstha has started Engineering College with Civil, Mechanical, Electrical, and Computer and Electronics & Telecommunication specialization. So, Sanstha provided from KG to PG Education in the rural area.

Dr. Sanjay A. Deokar

Principal

Sharadchandra Pawar College of Engineering and Technology (formerly know as Someshwar Engineering college) has established in the year 2012, spread over 7.5 acre of land with hostels and canteen facilities. College is running four under graduate courses which are Computer Engineering, Civil Engineering, Electrical Engineering and Mechanical Engineering. College has taken up a drive to provide students centric education in order to produce industry ready engineers.
